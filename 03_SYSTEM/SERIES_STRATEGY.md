# Series Strategy

## Audience Model
The strongest long-form retention pattern for this format is a competent antihero who can win, lose, and change over time.

## Why this works
- wins feel earned
- losses create discussion
- moral ambiguity encourages comments
- competence keeps viewers watching

## Episode Formula
1. Hook
2. Threat reveal
3. Tactical response
4. Costly success or failure
5. New consequence

## Release Mix
- Shorts for reach
- Episodes for retention
- Season compilations for session time
