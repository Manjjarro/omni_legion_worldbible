# 🎨 STYLE GUIDE

## Canonical Visual Style
* sharp 2D cell-shaded anime style
* bold ink contours
* flat color blocks
* high-contrast lighting
* minimal gradients
* cinematic keyframe finish

## Prompt Rules
* Keep prompts compact.
* Keep identity descriptors in character nodes.
* Keep world logic in WORLD_STATE.
* Keep camera language in scenes and shots.
* Put motion in shot notes, not character notes.

## Avoid
* Randomly changing eye color, hair color, or wardrobe
* Mixing too many camera moves in one shot
* Rewriting world rules inside prompts
* Repeating the same descriptor too often
