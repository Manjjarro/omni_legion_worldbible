# Brand Guide

## Brand Promise
Grimverse delivers dark, cinematic, character-driven conflict where wins always carry cost.

## Tone Words
- severe
- focused
- relentless
- atmospheric
- fatalistic
- stylish

## Thumbnail / Title Approach
- High contrast faces or silhouettes
- One clear threat
- One emotional hook
- One consequence

## Naming Direction
Best-fit titles should feel like:
- Grimverse
- Ashborn Protocol
- Obsidian Pulse
- Dead Signal

Avoid overly playful or generic naming.
