# Universe Rules: The Genesis Pulse & Gene Cradles

## Logline
In a post-apocalyptic dark fantasy world transformed by horrific biological mutations, a pragmatic survivor named Ren utilizes the ruthless progression mechanics of organic Gene Cradles to evolve his physical form and command a strike force known as the Omni Legion.

## Central Premise
Earth has been converted into a bloody ruin of flesh, rust, and mutation following an event known as the Genesis Pulse. Rather than relying on gamified digital UI menus or floating text popups (which warp in AI image generation), the apocalypse is completely organic. Evolution, ranks, and world progression are driven entirely through visceral body horror, environmental transformations, and mutating flesh pods called Gene Cradles.

## Core World-Building Rules

1. **The Art Direction Constant:** Every visual asset generated for this series must strictly adhere to the locked aesthetic style. Never mix styles or test random models. 
2. **The Organic Evolution Law:** Human and environmental mutations scale across an explicit 9-Tier Progression System. Physical growth, new traits, and structural defenses manifest visibly through organic textures rather than text or user interfaces.
3. **The Split-Wave Doctrine:** The Omni Legion operates as a highly coordinated, cold, and calculated frontline force. They deploy in staggered, mechanical waves to maximize kinetic pressure while maintaining clinical efficiency.
4. **Flesh Zone Corruption:** When a Gene Cradle fails or remains stagnant, it acts as zero-point infection vector. The surrounding geography corrupts into "Flesh Zones" where concrete turns into blood-red moss, tree bark mirrors human skin, and mechanical matter fuses directly with mutated bone and chitin.
