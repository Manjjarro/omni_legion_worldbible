## Location Brief

**Used by:** SCENE_004+ (EP_002)

**Visual Description (Background Plate source):**
A collapsed cathedral-observatory: a vaulted gothic nave whose domed roof has been
sheared away, exposing a ring of broken support ribs against a night sky. The
central floor is a sunken brass orrery platform, frozen mid-rotation, its rings
canted at odd angles. Tall shattered stained-glass windows line both walls —
most panes gone, the surviving fragments tinted deep violet and cold steel-grey.
Moonlight enters through the open dome and through the broken windows in hard,
angular shafts, throwing long-edged shadows across drifted ash and rubble.
Time of day: deep night. Atmosphere: still air, fine ash falling continuously
like dark snow, faint cold mist pooling at floor level around the orrery's base.

**Art Direction Constant (applies to every plate generated from this location):**
sharp 2D cell-shaded anime style, bold hand-drawn ink contours, flat color blocks,
high-contrast lighting, 0% 3D gradients, MAPPA/Wit Studio keyframe finish.

**Continuity Notes:**
- Stained-glass accent color is locked to violet (#6A0DAD family) and steel-grey
  (#C0C0C0 family) — matches CHAR_002 (Seraph) identity_anchor palette. Do not
  introduce warm-colored glass.
- Ash-fall density and moonlight shaft angles must remain consistent across all
  shots in SCENE_004+; treat moonlight direction as fixed (entering from
  screen-left/upper).
- The orrery platform's canted rings are a fixed prop — their tilt angle should
  not change shot-to-shot unless a scripted event (e.g. combat impact) alters it,
  in which case the new orientation becomes the new continuity baseline and must
  be logged.
- No artificial/emergency lighting sources in this location — all illumination is
  moonlight + ambient mist scatter. Any added light source (spell effects, etc.)
  must be scripted, not incidental.
