# Shot 005

## Action
Ren rounds the corridor bend and stops. A Null Choir operative stands at the far end — not advancing, just watching. Seraph's voice arrives in Ren's earpiece: "Don't. He's a signal, not a threat. Let him see you and move." Ren holds. The operative recedes into the smoke. Ren finds the faction marking carved into the breaker panel. The confirmation lands.

## Camera
Over-the-shoulder from behind Ren's left side — we see both Ren (foreground, sharp) and the operative (background, soft-focused in smoke). Camera holds static, no push, letting the stillness do the work. On the operative's retreat, a slow rack-focus pulls from background to the breaker panel mark in the mid-ground.

## Motion
Smoke from a smoldering cable coils between Ren and the operative. The operative's coat moves as they retreat — the only motion in an otherwise static frame. Ren's weapon hand is visible but still. Rack focus on the faction mark is the final motion.

## Composition
Hard foreground/background separation. Ren's silhouette as a stable anchor. The threat recedes — the frame empties deliberately. The faction marking in the mid-ground becomes the compositional payoff.

## Audio
Complete silence for two beats after Ren stops. Seraph's line — analogue-scratched, low volume. The operative's footsteps receding. Then the sound of Ren's gloved hand pressing against the panel — a confirmation beat. Sub-bass resolves briefly, then rebuilds.
