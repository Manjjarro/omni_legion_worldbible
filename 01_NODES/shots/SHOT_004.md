# Shot 004

## Action
Ren moves forward through the collapsed maintenance corridor, knife low, weight forward. Every few meters a severed cable spits a spark burst — brief illumination, then dark. Ash drifts horizontally, pushed by the pressure differential from the collapsed ventilation system ahead.

## Camera
Forward tracking shot behind and slightly below Ren — we follow at shoulder height as the corridor's parallel lines compress toward a vanishing point. Ren is never centred; he hugs the left wall, keeping his back shielded.

## Motion
Sparks fall in clusters from overhead cable bundles. Jacket edges flutter with each spark-burst air displacement. Ash drifts in flat horizontal streams, cutting through the frame left-to-right. Ren's movement is deliberate — no running, controlled advance.

## Composition
The corridor creates natural leading lines converging on Ren's destination. His silhouette against the sparks provides strong contrast. The visual tunnel builds pressure without showing the threat.

## Audio
Near-silence: footsteps on wet concrete, distant structural groan, spark bursts as punctuation. Seraph's voice arrives on analogue static mid-shot, a single word. Sub-bass tension builds on the second half of the shot.
