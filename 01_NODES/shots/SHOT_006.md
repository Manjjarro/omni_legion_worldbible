# Shot 006

## Action
Ren stands in the flooded underpass, knee-deep water around him, facing the civilian contractor. Seraph's instruction has just landed. He doesn't move for two full seconds. Then he acts. The frame stays on his face for one beat after — nothing changes in his expression. That's the point. The stillness is the characterisation: emotionally contained to the point where the cost is only visible in retrospect.

## Camera
Static close-medium on Ren from the front — chest to just above the head. No camera movement at all. The stillness of the frame contrasts with the action happening. Emergency red glow from ceiling strips pulses at irregular intervals, the only visual rhythm in the shot.

## Motion
Emergency glow pulses (battery-powered, non-networked — the single powered exception in Sector 7 per EVENT_001). Water drips from a broken pipe above — hits the flooded floor, rings out in the shallow water around Ren's boots. Ren's micro-hesitation (2 seconds) is the key motion event. After: his jaw sets. That is all.

## Composition
Tight frame creates psychological pressure. The red glow is the only colour source — everything else is grey and black. Ren's face takes up the centre third. Seraph is heard, not seen — her voice is present but she stays off-frame, making Ren carry the weight alone visually.

## Audio
Seraph's instruction — flat, unambiguous, analogue static. Two beats of complete silence. Then Ren's single movement. Water sound. Then silence again. No score. No ambient bed. Just the consequence landing in quiet.
