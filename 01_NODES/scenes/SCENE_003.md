# Scene 003 — The Cost of Winning

## Narrative Action
Ren reaches the flooded underpass — the designated exit point. The Null Choir has left one asset behind: a civilian contractor used as a living tripwire, bound to an analogue detonator. Seraph's choice is cold and immediate: neutralise the contractor or lose the exit. She gives Ren the decision, which is her own kind of cruelty. Ren acts. The exit is cleared. The win is real. But the choice has already changed how he's seen — and how he sees himself. He carries it out of the underpass with the same face he wore going in. That stillness is the tell.

## Visual Plan
Flooded floor reflects the emergency red glow from ceiling-mounted emergency strips — the only powered light source still alive on battery backup. Fog rolls low across the water surface. Broken signage overhead: a Sector 7 municipal marker, half-submerged. Just the colour of cost.
