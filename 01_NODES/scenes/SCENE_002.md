# Scene 002 — Ash in the Wiring

## Narrative Action
Ren presses forward through a collapsed maintenance corridor, the Pulse's aftershock still crackling through dead cable clusters overhead. Every shadow could be a Null Choir spotter. Seraph feeds intel through a shielded analogue line — the only communication surviving the blackout. At the corridor's midpoint, Ren locates a faction marking carved into a breaker panel: confirmation that the enemy leader has been through here within hours. The goal becomes a target.

## Visual Plan
Low, claustrophobic framing. The corridor walls press in — asymmetric silhouettes from hanging cables and debris. Sparks fall intermittently from severed conduits, each burst illuminating Ren's face for a split second before darkness reasserts. The ash drifts are horizontal here, pushed by ventilation collapse. Key visual motif: Ren's silhouette moving through sparks, knife drawn but low.

## Audio Plan
Near silence broken by: electrical crackling from severed cables, the distant metallic groan of the failing structure, Seraph's voice scratched by analogue static, Ren's footsteps — deliberate, near-silent. Sub-bass hum builds as the corridor narrows. No music until SHOT_005 resolution.
