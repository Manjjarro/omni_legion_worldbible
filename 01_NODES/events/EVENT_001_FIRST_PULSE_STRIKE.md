# Event 001 — First Pulse Strike on Sector 7

## What Happened
The Genesis Pulse detonated silently across Sector 7's grid, dropping every networked system within a three-block radius. Ren and Seraph were mid-approach when the collapse hit. Ren absorbed a peripheral Pulse bleed through contact with a live conduit — not enough to break him, but enough to leave a mark.

## Consequences Applied
- **Ren**: Temporary fracture-glow visible in crimson eyes under extreme electrical exposure. This is cosmetic drift, **not** a forbidden identity change. Document for future continuity.
- **Grid**: Sector 7 is electrically dead for the duration of EP_001. Any shot using powered equipment in this zone is a continuity error.
- **Null Choir**: Gains strategic silence advantage. Their operatives can move without electronic detection.
- **Seraph**: First time her risk calculus overrides her protocol. She stays despite odds.

## Continuity Enforcement
Any prompt generation for shots in EP_001 / SCENE_001–003 must acknowledge:
- No functional electronics in Sector 7
- Ren's physical state: minor conduit contact residue, otherwise peak combat readiness
- Atmospheric: ash drift, sparking dead cables, fog from collapsed ventilation
