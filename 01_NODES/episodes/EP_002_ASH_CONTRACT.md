# Episode 002 — Ash Contract

## Episode Brief
Ren surfaces from the Sector 7 collapse with a name: the Null Choir's liaison to Sector 7 is a contract broker known as "Ash." He's not a fighter — he's the mechanism that turned the civilian contractor into a tripwire. Ren's next move is to find Ash before the Iron Veil does. Both factions want the same asset. Ren gets there first by not playing by either faction's rules.

## Thematic Function
This episode pays off the moral cost established in EP_001 SCENE_003. The world begins to treat Ren as someone capable of that kind of decision — which opens doors and closes others. His reputation becomes a tool and a constraint simultaneously.

## Emotional Arc
Starts cold (controlled dread from EP_001 aftermath), escalates to tactical pressure (confrontation with Ash's network), ends with grim clarity (Ren learns what Ash contracted him for — and why it was always going to be him).
