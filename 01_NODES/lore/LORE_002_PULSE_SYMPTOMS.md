# Pulse Symptoms

## Truth
The Genesis Pulse causes intermittent failures, sensory distortion, and behavioral stress in exposed zones.

## Implications
- People become unreliable under pressure
- Machines fail at critical moments
- The world feels hostile and unstable
- Characters are forced into direct confrontation
