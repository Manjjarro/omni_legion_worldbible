# UNIVERSE RULES & CORE MECHANICS

## 1. The Split-Wave Doctrine (The Pulse)
* **The Rule:** The "Genesis Pulse" behaves as a deterministic, global environmental sweep. It instantly alters atmospheric physics.
* **Visual Anchor:** The sky is permanently locked to low-saturation slate tones; neon atmospheric bleeding only occurs along localized fault lines or when an active entity triggers progression evolution.
* **Prompt Restriction:** No clear blue skies, no warm ambient sunlight, no organic floral landscapes unless explicitly corrupted by Flesh Moss.

## 2. Power Progression Systems (The 9-Tier System)
* **The Rule:** Leveling up is purely biological and body-horror driven. Text blocks, floating holographic screens, and glowing video-game numerical menus are **completely banned** to prevent word-warping AI bugs.
* **Visual Signifiers:**
  - **Tier 1:** Standard human anatomy, tactical fabrics, weapon reliance.
  - **Tier 2 (The Chitin Overlap):** Hard, segmented obsidian chitin plates wrap symmetrically over muscle paths (forearms, shoulders).
  - **Tier 4 (Neural Integration):** Deep bio-luminescent crimson neural lines visible beneath skin fissures along the jaw and throat line.

## 3. Environmental Limits (Flesh Zones)
* **The Rule:** When an evolutionary core fails, the local zone undergoes rapid environmental mutation. Concrete decays into organic tissue vectors.
* **Material Tiers:** "Concrete-flesh fusion, calcified bone debris, industrial rust patterns".
